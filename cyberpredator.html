Who are Cyber Predators?

A Cyber Predator is an adult who uses the internet to exploit children and/or teens with the intention of inflicting sexual, emotional, psychological, or financial abuse.

How do they lure kids into their traps?

Cyber Predators connect with children and teens through chat rooms, instant messaging, social networks, video games, and more. They often pretend to be a child or teen and use their online connections to manipulate and groom victims to gain their trust over time. Cyber Predators work to build a relationship with online victims, then use that relationship to take advantage of unsuspecting children and teens.

Is anything being done against cyber predators?

Yes.Cyber Predators can be sent to prison if caught and convicted of crimes against children. However, predators cannot be caught if they aren't reported.
